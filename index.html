A Look into HIV/AIDS in NYC Today

KEY FINDINGS - NYC 5 BOROUGHS*

PLWHA = People Living with HIV/AIDS
  • There are over 120,000 people in the 5 boroughs living with HIV/AIDS
  • The number of PLWHA has increased an average of 1.4% each year for the last 5 years
  • New York leads the nation in the number of new HIV cases
  • HIV is the third leading cause of death for NYC residents ages 35 to 54
  • In NYC, 50% of HIV-positive New Yorkers are over fifty
  • In 2014, over 60% of new HIV Diagnoses in NYC were among 20-39 year olds

*Data takes awhile to collect so after year-end, it takes almost two years for all the statistics and records to be updated. This Data is based on 2009-2014 records.
